&styler; improves both the look and the functionality of eComStation.

&styler; resides in the &link_lookfdr;.

It allows to use bitmaps or shades for window title bars, choose among various styles for the pushbuttons, assign useful functions to the Windows keys of your keyboard, and enhance the system shutdown to put the system in power saving mode, power it off or reboot a different partition.
