A domain is a logical grouping of several computers in a network.
  • Local Area Networks (LANs) use domains to organize servers and clients. You specify the domain that you are part of when you install &os2;.

    Open the &link_wpnetgrp; to see all servers that are part of your domain.

  • &link_tcpip; networks use domains too. Domains are then typically used in the form of www.ecomstation.com. This specifies the www computer, which is part of the ecomstation.com network. This type of domain names is most frequently used in &link_internet; addresses.
