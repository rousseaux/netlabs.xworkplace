On this notebook page you can remove certain default items from the context menus of all folders by unchecking the respective check box. Changing the settings will take effect immediately.

Press "Undo" to restore the settings that were in use before you opened this notebook page.

Press "Default" to restore the settings to the installation state.

The items which can be removed are grouped together according to the following:

  • The "Folder menu items" group affects folders only. Since disk objects (root folders) are folders too, they will be affected as well.

    Note: "Sort" and "Arrange" have no effect on the Desktop because the Desktop has its own settings for these two menu items in its own properties notebook.

  • The "Disk menu items" affect disk objects (root folders) only.

  • The "Common menu items" will affect all objects on your system, be they folders, disk objects, programs, data files or whatever.
