The "Virtual desktop area" on the middle of the page shows you the layout of your virtual desktops as set by the X and Y sliders to the left and the top of the area.

The &pgr; startup desktop is highlited. This is the desktop that &pgr; will use when it starts up. You can change the startup desktop by double-clicking on one of the desktops.

The default initial desktop is at the top-left corner.
