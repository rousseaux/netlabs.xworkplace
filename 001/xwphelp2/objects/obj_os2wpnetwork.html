The "&connectfdr;" folder is created by &os2; to allow you to connect to other computers on a local area network (LAN). Such networks are used in both small and large networks and group computers into domains.

For example, if your computer is part of a company network, you will probably use LAN technology to be able to see resources from a central server. But you can also build a small LAN at your own home, for example to connect your laptop to your desktop machine.
