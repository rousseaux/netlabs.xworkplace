The "&sysfdr;" folder groups objects that are related to the &os2; operating system and your hardware in one way or the other. This is a special folder created by &os2;. By default, among others, this contains:

  • the &link_cmdfdr; to open various command prompts;
  • the &link_syssetupfdr;, which contains, among others, objects for manipulating your mouse, keyboard, and Workplace Shell settings;
  • the &link_drivesfdr;, which allows you to access all files on your system.
Double-click on the "&sysfdr;" folder icon to display the objects in the folder.
