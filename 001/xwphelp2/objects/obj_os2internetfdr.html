The "Internet" folder is created by &os2; to allow you to connect to other computers using Internet technology, also known as TCP/IP.

It contains programs for dialing into an Internet Service Provider (ISP) and standard Internet utilities such as FTP, ping, and Telnet. You can also use these utilities for computers in a local area network (LAN), if they are connected to your computer via TCP/IP as well.
