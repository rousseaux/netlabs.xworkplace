The "&xwp; Setup" object lets you configure the additional features &xwp; adds to your Workplace Shell. Depending on the settings in this object, &xwp; can replace or enhance the behavior of your Workplace Shell.

The "&xwp; Setup" object is created in your "System setup" folder. You will also find a shadow of that object in the default configuration folder so that it appears in the "&xwp;" submenu in each folder's context menu.
